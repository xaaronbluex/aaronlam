Wilderness level of 24 country parks in Hong Kong

The map shows the wilderness level of all 24 country parks in Hong Kong. The wilderness level is calculated with four factors: distance to road, landuse type, elevation, and visibility of human features. The wilderness level is divided into 5 categories: 0-20%, 20-40%, 40-60%, 60-80%, and 80-100%. The map is created by using ArcGIS Desktop 10.6 and the data is obtained from the Hong Kong government. The map is designed to illustrate the wilderness levels of each country park in Hong Kong and to offer information for conservation and restoration efforts.
